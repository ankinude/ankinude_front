WHAT

Is it possible to create a space where people feel good emotions only, become free and one with everything?

This is the question that motivates us, that we explore both scientifically and experimentally, and from which ANKINUDE dynamically and creatively unfolds.

OUR MISSION

We e create a place where people feel accepted as they are and can let go of all limitations to contribute to more connectedness in this world.

We work with interactive, dynamic art forms that play with the particular structure and unfolding possibilities of the respective environment. With the participants at the center, magic is created through experimental exploration, and spontaneous reactive part-being. The audience simultaneously creates and jointly permeates their own experiential space in the actual moment.
